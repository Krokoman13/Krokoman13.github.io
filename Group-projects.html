The Ressurection

The Ressurection is a point and click game where you attempt to perform a ritual that could ressurect your dead wife.

As part of my study I worked together with a Group of students to make a point and click game in Java. I was the main programmaer for this game, it was made from scatch.

Six Eyes of Darkness

Six Eyes of Darkness is a colaborative boardgame where 4 heroes explore a randomly generated dungeon and try to beat the Hydra that lives within.

As part of my study I worked together with a Group of students to make a board game, I was the all around worker trying to help everyone so it could be done in time.
