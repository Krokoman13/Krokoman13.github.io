Group projects

Becouse of the my study I work with a lot of teams, personally I really love the feeling of working together and making something awesome! I am very proud of all of these so I posted them on this site.

Souls of Steel - Second Engineer

Souls of Steel is a cooperative stealth game set in Mariupol Ukraine. It is meant to let the player experiance what it is like to live in Ukraine during war.


My main responsibilities where networking, ai/enemies and stealth mechanics.


I was honored to have been asked for this project, and with a full team of 12 people we worked on 2 games for 9 weeks (this game was made in conjunction with Bound by Bonds).

Bound by Bonds - Head Engineer

Bound by Bonds is a 2 player interactive story experiance. It is meant to let the player experiance what it is like to live in Ukraine during war.


This project was done in conjunction with Souls of Steel, it was my responsibility to port and alter all code from that game to this game and develop tools for the designers of Bound by Bonds.


I was honored to have been asked for this project, and with a full team of 12 people we worked on 2 games for 9 weeks (this game was made in conjunction with Souls of Steel).

2 Meter Mayhem - Enviroment/NPC Programmer

As part of a school project, we were tasked with making a game that could be part of an ad campaign. One of our team members thought of "Please give me space", a campaign to politely promote social distancing.


So we made a game about how hard it is to shop in a grocery store and keep an eye on all other shoppers.


My job was to make the store come alive, with groceries you could remove from the shelves, shoppers that wander around, and a friend that tries to reach you and does not respect your personal space.
