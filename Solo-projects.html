Alone in space

Alone in space is a game about flying through space with somewhat realistic physics (frictionless) and you have to dodge or shoot incoming projectiles.

As part of my study I programmed this game in a custom engine (C# based), I mostly focused on Making it fun to control and learning how the engine works and how I could modify it.

SpellThief

SpellThief is a top-down game where the player explores a dungeon and acquires “Spellbooks” the player can cast spells based on their acquired Spellbooks. These Spellbooks are essential to overcoming the challenges in the dungeon, which vary from puzzles to combat.

As part of my study I had to make this game in Flowlab which was an interesting challange.

It can be played here.
