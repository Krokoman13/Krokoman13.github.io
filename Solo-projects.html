Solo projects

Like most people who want to be creative: I have a lot of solo projects. Most of the finished ones I made becouse of/through my study, some of the most interesting ones I posted on this site.

To learn more about these click "Solo projects" at the top of the screen.

Tile Island Generator

A tool to generate tile based islands for a survival island escape game.

Designed partially together with a disigner, with many settings for them to play with.

KrokEngine - 2D Game engine, Physics

My own C++ 2D Game Engine with basic phyhsics. As a challenge and a learning experiance I tried making a 2D engine and adding some physics to it.

The purpose was to learn more about memory menagment and ownership. Next to this I wanted to try impliment 2D collision handeling for shapes considing of lines and circles.

Chess Mimic - My chess program

The idea is that the program is able to sift through existing chess games between professionals or from advanced Chess Ai’s and remember what good moves are for specific board states.

This should be more efficient and easy than making an AI capable of calculating the good moves in real time.

Alone in Space - Custom game engine

Alone in space is a game about flying through space with somewhat realistic physics (frictionless) and you have to dodge or shoot incoming projectiles.

As part of my study I programmed this game in a custom engine (C# based), I mostly focused on Making it fun to control and learning how the engine works and how I could modify it.

Tank Battle - Custom physics engine

The goal of this project was to make a custom physics engine with my own collision detection system.

To demonstrate that my engine works I was tasked to make a top down Tank shooting game.
