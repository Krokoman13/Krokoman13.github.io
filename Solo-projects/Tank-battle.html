Tank battle

The goal of this project was to make a custom physics engine with my own collision detection system.

To demonstrate that my engine works I was tasked to make a top down Tank shooting game.

To achieve this goal I learned the math behind 2D vectors, mainlly trigonomatry, and some laws of motion.

Moving rectangle physics where the hardest to acchieve, but in the end I acchieved it bye treating rectangles like four lines and 4 small circles.
