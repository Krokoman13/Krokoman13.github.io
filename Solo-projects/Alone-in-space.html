Alone in space

Alone in Space is a game about flying through space with somewhat realistic physics (frictionless) and you have to dodge or shoot incoming projectiles.

The goal of this project was to take a game engine (C# based), created by my teachers, and try to make it my own.

I mostly focused on Making it fun to control and learning how the engine works and how I could modify it.

During this project I learned how much I enjoy figuring out complex systems and improving them.
